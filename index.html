Research interests

Machine learning applied to multimedia. Emphasis on natural language processing (speech and text), spoken content retrieval and audio-visual biometrics.

Research Experience

  • Researcher, LIMSI-CNRS (December 2011 - present)
    • End-to-end development of large vocabulary continuous speech recognition systems (>1 million words), including acoustic and language modeling, tuning and testing in multiple languages.
    • Application of linguistic content of speech for person recognition in TV shows.
    • Creation of comparable corpora by automatically linking Web-derived text descriptions with speech content from TV series for spoken content retrieval.
    • Speaker-aware spoken content retrieval.
    • Development and testing of speaker verification systems, participation in NIST SRE.
    • Research projects: OSEO Quaero, ANR REPERE, BABEL, CAMOMILE.

  • Research Assistant, Idiap Research Institute, Martigny, Switzerland (2007-2011)
    • Development of novel machine learning approaches and localized feature representations for speech and speaker recognition.
    • Study of novel approaches for cross- and multimodal biometrics, and face detection.
